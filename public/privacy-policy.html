Privacy Policy - Lucid AI Memory Extension

Last Updated: December 2024
Effective Date: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lucid AI Memory ("we," "our," or "the Extension"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the Lucid AI Memory browser extension.

2. Information We Collect

The Lucid AI Memory Extension collects the following information with your explicit consent:

  • ChatGPT Conversations: Text content of your ChatGPT interactions when the extension is active and you are authenticated. We only capture conversations after you connect your wallet, indicating explicit consent.
  • Wallet Information: Your Solana wallet public address (NOT private keys or seed phrases). We never have access to or store your private keys.
  • User Identity: A unique user ID generated by our authentication provider (Privy) for account management.
  • Usage Statistics: Number of messages processed, tokens earned, session duration, and interaction patterns.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, extension version, error logs for debugging and improving the extension.
  • Transaction Data: Records of mGas token rewards earned and distributed through the Lucid L2 blockchain.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Process AI conversations through the Lucid L2 network for reward calculation
  • Calculate and distribute mGas token rewards based on your ChatGPT usage
  • Track achievements and progress in the rewards system
  • Improve extension functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive behavior
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement multiple layers of data protection:

  • Local Storage: Wallet information and user preferences are stored securely in your browser's Chrome storage (chrome.storage.local). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted without your action.
  • Remote Storage: Conversation data and rewards information are stored on Lucid L2 servers with industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) both in transit and at rest.
  • Blockchain Storage: Transaction records and reward distributions are permanently stored on the Solana blockchain, which is a public, decentralized ledger.
  • Access Controls: We implement strict access controls to limit who can access your data internally.
  • Security Measures: We use firewalls, encryption, and regular security audits to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Privy (privy.io): Secure wallet authentication and management. Privy handles the connection between your wallet and our extension. Privy Privacy Policy
  • Solana Blockchain: Transaction processing and token distribution. Blockchain data is public by design. Solana Privacy Policy
  • WalletConnect: Wallet connectivity protocol for enhanced wallet support. WalletConnect Privacy Policy

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We only share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With the third-party services listed above, which are necessary for extension functionality.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Lucid, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified).

We never share your private keys or seed phrases because we never have access to them.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data by contacting us at privacy@lucid.foundation
  • Deletion: Delete your local data via extension settings → Reset Data button
  • Wallet Disconnection: Disconnect your wallet at any time, which stops new data collection
  • Stop Capture: Disable the extension to stop conversation capture
  • Data Export: Request a machine-readable copy of your data
  • Account Deletion: Request complete account deletion by contacting privacy@lucid.foundation
  • Opt-Out: Uninstall the extension to completely stop all data collection

Important Note: Blockchain data (transaction records) cannot be deleted due to the immutable nature of blockchain technology.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Local Storage: Retained until you clear it, reset the extension, or uninstall
  • Server Data: Retained as long as your account is active, plus 90 days after account deletion for legal compliance
  • Blockchain Data: Permanently stored on the Solana blockchain (cannot be deleted)
  • Backup Data: Securely stored backups are deleted after 30 days
  • Logs: System logs are retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes

9. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@lucid.foundation, and we will delete such information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Compliance with GDPR for European users
  • Compliance with CCPA for California residents

11. Cookies and Tracking

The Lucid AI Memory Extension does not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies. We only use Chrome's local storage API to save your preferences and wallet information on your device.

12. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For material changes, we will notify you through the extension or via email
  • Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Right to delete personal information (with exceptions)
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@lucid.foundation

14. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@lucid.foundation

  • Explain the steps we're taking to address the breach

  • Comply with all applicable data breach notification laws
