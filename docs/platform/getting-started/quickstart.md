# Quickstart

Get up and running with Lucid in under 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Sign Up

Visit [lucid.foundation](https://lucid.foundation) and create your account. You can sign up with email, Google, or a crypto wallet via Privy authentication.

## Step 2: Create a Workspace

After signing in, you'll be prompted to create your first workspace. A workspace is your team's shared environment — it holds your agents, plugins, knowledge base, and settings.

Give your workspace a name and an optional description. You can create additional workspaces later from the sidebar.

## Step 3: Create Your First Agent

Navigate to **Assistants** in the sidebar and click **+ New Assistant**. You'll need:

- **Name** — A display name for your agent (e.g., "Support Bot", "Trading Analyst")
- **System prompt** — Instructions that define your agent's personality and behavior
- **Model** — Choose from 100+ AI models across providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, and more)

Click **Create** and your agent is ready to test.

## Step 4: Test Your Agent

Open your agent's detail page and use the built-in **Test Chat** panel to send messages. Your agent will respond using the model and instructions you configured.

## Step 5: Connect a Channel

To deploy your agent to real users, connect a messaging channel:

1. Go to your agent's **Channels** tab
2. Click **Add Channel**
3. Choose a platform (Telegram, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp, Microsoft Teams, iMessage, or Web)
4. Follow the setup guide for that platform

Once connected, your agent will automatically respond to messages on that channel.
