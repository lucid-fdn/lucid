# Plans and Limits

Canonical launch pricing and runtime model live in [runtime-model-matrix.md](./runtime-model-matrix.md).

## Launch Plans

### Free

Shared sandbox for trying the product.

### Starter

$29/month for managed shared compute aimed at solo builders.

### Growth

$99/month or $79/month billed yearly for production shared runtime usage.

### Scale

Starting at $299/month for an isolated runtime with stronger continuity and headroom.

### Enterprise

Custom pricing for private cloud, partner cloud, compliance, and custom operations.
